# Qadam – IT Career Launch Platform

> A centralized platform for students and beginners in Kazakhstan to find internships, courses, events, and mentorship – all in one place.

##  Problem Statement

Entering the IT industry is challenging for students and career‑switchers:
- Scattered information about internships and training.
- No clear path to build a portfolio and connect with employers.
- Lack of personalized guidance.

**Qadam** solves this by providing a single hub where candidates can discover opportunities, learn new skills, and get hired.

##  Features

- **Internships** – Browse and apply to curated internships with salary filters and match scores.
- **Courses** – Free and paid courses from local providers.
- **Events** – Hackathons, meetups, and workshops calendar.
- **Mentorship** – Connect with experienced IT professionals.
- **Student Dashboard** – Manage applications, saved items, portfolio, and CV.
- **Employer Dashboard** – Post internships, courses, events, and review applicants.
- **Firebase Authentication** – Secure sign‑up / sign‑in.
